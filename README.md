## About me
I’m an **Assistant Professor** in **Industrial & Management Engineering** at **Hanyang University ERICA (Ansan, Korea)** and lead the **Industrial Operations & Data Analytics (IODA) Lab**.  
My work focuses on **AI + data-driven industrial analytics** for **smart manufacturing** and **reliability engineering**, including **PHM/predictive maintenance**, **RAMS**, **semiconductor manufacturing optimization**, and **battery SOH/RUL modeling**.  
**Lab:** https://iodalab.lovable.app/

## Research Themes
- **Predictive Maintenance based on PHM:** Condition monitoring and predictive maintenance technologies based on Prognostics and Health Management (PHM)
- **AI Optimization for Smart Manufacturing:** AI-based operational optimization for smart manufacturing systems
- **Explainable Smart Manufacturing:** Improving transparency and reliability of smart manufacturing decisions using Explainable AI
- **RAMS Analysis:** Enhancing Reliability, Availability, Maintainability, and Safety (RAMS) of industrial systems
- **Battery analytics:** SOH / RUL modeling, BMS & energy storage optimization
